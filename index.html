Ghana

Ghana, located on the West African coast, is a culturally diverse nation with a rich history and a stable democracy since gaining independence in 1957. Its borders are defined by Cote d'Ivoire to the west, Burkina Faso to the north, and Togo to the east, while the south faces the Gulf of Guinea. These borders encompass a land of contrasts, from the lush forests of the south to the arid savannahs in the north, making it a geographically fascinating country. Ghana's friendly people, historic forts and castles, vibrant traditions, and stunning natural landscapes make it a captivating destination for travelers seeking both cultural immersion and breathtaking scenery..
